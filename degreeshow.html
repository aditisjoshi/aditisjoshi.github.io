EXHIBITION

displaying work from our masters cohort

Along with a team of several students, I was involved in helping put together our final degree show to show our masters' work. I was involved with branding, catalogue, and exhibition space design. The show displayed the work of 63 students from 7 different courses. We decided on a grid layout so that each person could have their own area and space for displaying work and prototypes but also so that there was a consistent narrative and visual language throughout the space. The exhibition was titled 'Possibilities' to show the diverse range of projects and their future-facing nature.
